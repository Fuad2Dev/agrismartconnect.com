FieldIQ™ On-Farm

FieldIQ™ On-Farm Features:
• Store all field data in one consistent place
• Store soil fertility information
• Use any and all of your data to create prescriptions – fertility, seeding, and more
• Sync with location-aware mobile applications
• Compare multiple layers of your data for deeper analysis
• Create customized data-driven management zones
• Import data from multiple sources including MyJohnDeere, Climate FieldView, and a wide variety of field monitors
• Develop crop plans with multiple scenarios to identify your season’s most profitable strategy

FieldIQ™ Full-Service

FieldIQ™ Full-Service includes all the features from On-Farm, plus:
• Operation set-up and management
• One-on-one strategy sessions
• Data review for quality control
• Data cleaning/reconciliation
• Custom fertility and seeding prescriptions
• Data entry and crop records management
• Tailored soil and nutrition analysis
• Personalized agronomic insights
• High quality management zone creation
• End of season financial analysis
• End of season landowner reports
