All Business Solutions

Structure and Succession Planning

Our structure experts can help you ensure your farm's entity structure provides maximum benefit and protection to you as owners. We'll also help you plan a smooth transition for your farming operation from one generation to the next to protect your farm's legacy.

Management and Leadership

UnCommon Farms resources guide you through a thorough assessment of your current operation, identifying opportunities for improved management and efficiency. Training courses help you gain the skills you need for maximum effectiveness.

Financials

We will help you analyze your current financials and compare them to your operation's historical performance, then guide you to use that knowledge for improved managerial decisions to improve profitability, reduce costs, and secure financial assistance.

Human Resources

UnCommon Farms resources will help you clearly define the roles and responsibilities of your workforce, bolster your employee recruiting strategy, retain good workers, and develop policies and procedures to ensure compliance with HR regulations.

Networking / Peer Groups

Peer groups are a great way to learn and advance your family farm by networking and learning from fellow like-minded farms. The learning does not stop when the meeting concludes. Our peer group members act as a support system outside of the meetings, sharing information and best practices to help their fellow members succeed.

Farm-Focused Peer Groups

Learning from other producers through peer groups can provide valuable knowledge to move your farm forward. Since problems and solutions are not one-size-fits-all and every team member should be learning and growing, we offer a wide range of peer groups for members of your farm operation. General Managers

This program will provide you with the management skills and knowledge to lead your farming operation now and as it grows. We discuss the latest trends in ag, bring in guest speakers on current topics of interest in the industry and provide farm tours and benchmarking opportunities!

Management Development

Operate your farm more efficiently to increase productivity, profitability, and morale with our management training. We’ll provide experts to speak on current topics of interest and agribusiness tours that allow you to explore how other farms operate, providing valuable insight into best practices from around the country! Our benchmarking allows you to measure your performance against that of similar farms.

Operations Managers

You have one of the most comprehensive operations manager training programs at your fingertips! You’ll learn how to be more efficient and effective on the job and how state-of-the-art farm technology can help improve operational efficiency. With this diverse range of learning options available for managers like you, we’re confident we can help you improve your farming operation.

Agronomy

In this group, you will find everything from soil health basics to advanced agronomic tactics, tools, and strategies. Whether you are a beginner or an expert, there is something here to broaden and deepen your agronomic knowledge and skills!

Financial

Understanding the finances of your farm is vital to its health and prosperity. That’s why this group focuses on every aspect of finance, from revenue streams to expenses, so you maintain a firm grasp on all the elements of your financial health. You can apply that knowledge to strategic managerial decisions that drive your success.

Women

What’s not to love about a group of strong female ag entrepreneurs? The women’s peer group is an invaluable resource for anyone who wants to learn more about ag, interact with other women involved in a farm, or compare notes with farm women pursuing other interests or careers. These ladies don’t just share their triumphs and challenges but also gain valuable insight into topics such as human resources, grain marketing, accounting, and business marketing!

Dads

This peer group is a place for fathers to share their farming wisdom and experiences as they prepare to transition into new territory. Transitioning management responsibilities and ownership to the next generation can be a challenge and a blessing! Join other fathers who have been through this before you; you can learn from their experiences to help ease your transition!

Canada

Canadian producers face their unique struggles. The Canada peer group brings together Canadian producers to benchmark finances, talk agronomics, tackle human resources challenges, and more with other producers.
