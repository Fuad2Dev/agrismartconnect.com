Learn From Your Peers

Farm-Focused Peer Groups

Peer groups are a great way to learn and advance your family farm by networking and learning from fellow like-minded farms. Subject-matter-focused peer groups give participants the opportunity to deep dive and learn from experts as well as from members of other operations that also have a focus in that area. The learning does not stop when the meeting concludes. Our peer group members act as a support system outside of the meetings, sharing information and best practices to help their fellow members succeed.

Contact Us to Join

General Managers

This program will provide you with the skills and knowledge to lead an entire farming operation. We bring in expert speakers to share their knowledge. We also provide farms tours and all-encompassing benchmarking learning opportunities.

Management Development

Operate your farm more efficiently to increase productivity, profitability, and morale with our management training. We’ll provide outside speakers for various topics of interest and agribusiness tours exploring how other farms operate to provide valuable insight into best practices from around the country! We’ll also cover benchmarking to help you measure your business against any competitors who might be close by.

Operations Managers

One of the most comprehensive operations manager training programs is right at your fingertips! You’ll learn how to be more efficient and effective on-the-job, as well as learn about available state-of-the-art farm technology that can help improve efficiency. With this diverse range of learning options for managers like yourself, we’re confident we can help you improve your farming operation.

Agronomy

In this group, you will find everything from soil health basics to advanced agronomic tactics and strategies. Whether it’s for beginners or experts in the field, there is something here that everyone can learn from!

Financial

Understanding the finances of your farm is vital to its health and prosperity. That’s why this group focuses on all aspects of finance, from revenue streams to expenses, so you can have the firm grasp on your financials you need to be successful!

Women

What’s not to love about a group of strong female ag entrepreneurs? The women’s peer group is an invaluable resource for anyone who wants advice or guidance in their career, as well as those seeking like-minded individuals. These ladies don’t just share their triumphs, but also offer valuable insight on topics such as grain marketing, accounting, and how to best handle business marketing!

Dads

The dad’s peer group is a place for fathers to share their farming wisdom and experiences as they make the transition into new territory. The transition to the next generation can be a challenge, but it is not impossible! Join together with other fathers who have been through this before you and find your support system – learning from others who share similar experiences makes for a smoother transition!

Canada

Canadian producers face their unique struggles. The Canada peer group brings together Canadian producers to benchmark finances, talk agronomics, tackle human resources challenges, and more.
